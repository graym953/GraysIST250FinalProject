Care Tips

  • Feed a balanced, high-quality diet.
  • Provide regular exercise appropriate for age and breed.
  • Keep up with vaccinations and vet visits.
  • Groom regularly to maintain a healthy coat and skin.
  • Offer love, attention, and mental stimulation daily.

Dog Life Stages

Stage Age Range Care Focus
Puppy 0-1 year Socialization, training, vaccinations
Adult 1-7 years Exercise, diet, vet checkups
Senior 7+ years Special diet, gentle exercise, joint care
